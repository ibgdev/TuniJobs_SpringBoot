Become a Partner Company

Would you like to post your job offers on TuniJobs? Join our network by becoming a partner company.

Steps to follow:

To request the creation of a company account, please send an email to the following address:

support-team@tunijobs.tn

The email must include the following information:

  • Company name
  • Tax Identification Number
  • Industry sector
  • Full address
  • Website
  • Phone number
  • Manager's email (the manager must have an active candidate account on the platform)
  • Logo URL or the logo as an attachment

Our team will contact you once your request has been reviewed.
